Chicken Tortillas

Chicken tortilla soup to warm your soul. Heart chicken cooked to perfection with spicy tortilla shells and seasonings.

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast
  • Larry's seasoning salt
  • hot peppers
  • corn
  • mexican shredded cheese
  • chicken broth

Steps:

  1. Put pan on medium heat with oil to warm. Cut hot peppers to wanted sized. Add to pan. Add corn to pan.
  2. Take chicken breast and cut into serving sizes. Then add to pan.
  3. Add some Larry's seasoning salt
  4. Allow chicken to cook until temp to 160o. Remove from pan to plate then add cheese.
